Bring the cozy farm clock to your wrist

This watchface recreates the look and feel of the beloved farming game with Pebble-ready pixel art inspired by the original.

Features:
- Digital date and time with font designs faithful to the original
- Sunrise and sunset indicator shows the arc of daylight for your location
- Current weather conditions and seasons are reflected on the clock
- Step count (on supported Pebble models) or current temperature, displayed in the lower panel (configurable in the Pebble app)
- Battery level displays as an energy bar when you shake your wrist twice
- Display a special season indicator image when it is your birthday! (Set this up in Configuration)
- All platforms supported, black and white, color, rectangular, round, big, and small!
- Timeline quick view works!

---
